Quality Assurance


We follow strict quality control policies to give our customers the quality products. Our adherence to ethical business practices and qualitative approach has given us an edge over the other leading organizations. Right from the initial stage of procurement of raw material till the final delivery of the consignment, we undertake stringent quality control measures.

Tour quality analysts stringently check our range of products on well-defined parameters such as:


  • Load capacity to ensure that the crates, pallets and waste bins can be used effectively to transport and store heavy items
  • We ensure that our plastic products are made corrosion resistant and resistant to acid and alkali solutions
  • Strength of our products is of vital importance, so we conduct various tests to ensure that they can withstand heavy weight


With the help of these quality control parameters, we have maintained a strong position in the industry. Our advanced methodology and innovative techniques enable us to manufacture our range of products in compliance with international norms. Further, we also update our testing machines and tools on regular bases so that we can provide effective plastic crates, pallets and waste bins to our valued clients.
